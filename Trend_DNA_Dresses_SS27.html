Season read

SS27 dresses turn romantic, fluid and craft-led. The defining moves: the deep front plunge as refined femininity, barely-there French lace and sheer tulle used as delicate accents, voluminous billowing volumes with cascading ruffles and high-low hems, and 3D floral embellishment for occasion. Color runs romantic — wine and oxblood depth beside a hazy pastel transition from Peach Fuzz into Soft Lilac.
