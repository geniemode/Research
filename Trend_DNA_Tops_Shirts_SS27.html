Season read

SS27 holds two energies in tension: warm, earthy depth (terracotta, clay, moss, wine) against cool, tech-forward brightness (teal, cobalt, electric lavender). Across tops the move is toward relaxed-but-structured shapes — boxy, cropped, breathable — and what forecasters call "quiet maximalism": richness through texture, hand-finishing and layering rather than loud excess. Structural breathability (open-weave, seersucker, heavyweight linen) replaces flimsy sheerness.
