Season read · SS27

SS27 menswear is soft, light and status-quiet. The Milan Men's Spring 2027 shows made linen "impossible to ignore" — no longer vacation-only but an on-and-off-duty status fabric, in low-wrinkle blends and soft, unconstructed, elongated shapes. WGSN's S/S 27 data agrees: linen, resort and camp-collar shirts rise as standard cotton softens. Around that core, two energy plays: soft resort (relaxed/boxy, camp collars, knit polos, resort-to-everyday) and a clear return of print after minimalism — still-life botanicals, paisley revival and dreamy diffused graphics — plus a heritage-craft / romantic-vintage test. Colour reads desert: pale yellow, sand, sun-faded neutrals, with Première Vision's "OPEN" brights as the pop.

Milan SS27: linen "impossible to ignore"

At Milan Men's Spring 2027, linen was the season's defining fabric — "renewed relevance" as heat reshapes dressing, now carrying "understated status" as the preferred on-and-off-duty fabric for executives, not just vacation. New blends cut the wrinkle; designers worked it from long-line overshirts to snap-front Western shirts.

SS27 fabric specifics: mercerized cotton-linen blends, checked jacquard weaves, super-light deconstructed/unlined natural cloth (linen, silk, cotton, seersucker).

Sources: WWD — Milan menswear Spring 2027 · MMS — SS27 shirt fabrics
Soft, unconstructed, elongated

SS27 tailoring softened: "softer constructions, fluid proportions and elongated silhouettes," unlined and deconstructed for ease. The shirt/overshirt follows — relaxed, boxy, lightweight — and WGSN's S/S 27 data confirms linen/resort/camp-collar share rising while plain cotton declines.

So: invest in linen + blends, soft collars, long-line layering; protect the core shirt and re-mix its fabric.

Sources: QART — 10 SS27 menswear trends · Monocle — best SS27 Milan · WGSN S/S 27 TrendCurve

The data-and-runway core: linen and its blends are SS27's status fabric. Protect the bestselling shirt, re-mix toward linen/camp-collar/long-line, and let soft, unconstructed, elongated shapes carry the newness.
