Season read

FW27 kidswear is cozy, warm and expressive. The core is knitwear and soft texture — chunky cable, fair-isle, faux-shearling and low-pile fur — in a warm earthy palette led by burgundy (the season's star) with terracotta, pine and cream. Around it sit three energies: bright playful colour-block outerwear, heritage preppy (plaid, corduroy, fair-isle, peacoats) and an expressive animal-print revival (leopard, faux-fur trims). Everything is fun-but-functional, gender-neutral and built for layering.
